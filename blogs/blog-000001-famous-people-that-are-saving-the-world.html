Famous People that are saving the world

  1. Leonardo DiCaprio
    • Leonardo DiCaprio is a very well known actor and film producer. But little do people know that he is also a huge environmentalist. In 1998, Leonardo DiCaprio founded The Leonardo DiCaprio Foundation as an effort to help address and respond to the growing climate crisis. His foundation has supported 132 organizations that are dedicated to the long-term health and wellbeing of all of Earth’s inhabitants and animals. He also joined the 2017 People’s Climate March which was a protest that took place in over 300 locations in and around the U.S. On top of that, in 2014, Leo spoke at the United Nations Climate Change Summit and was appointed as a Messenger of Peace. Not too long ago, DiCaprio donated $5 million through his new organization called Earth Alliance to help combat the Amazonian wildfires.
  2. Mark Ruffalo
    • American actor and producer Mark Ruffalo is not only the big green guy in movies, but is also trying to help make our environment more green. He founded the The Solutions Project which is an initiative supporting climate changemakers, innovators, and solutionaries that are determined to end the climate crisis.
  3. Shawn Mendes
    • Canada’s very own Shawn Mendes is also taking part in the effort to combat climate change. He has teamed up with Flow Alkaline Spring Water, which is a North American social water brand dedicated to reduce environmental footprint while providing the highest quality water. Shawn Mendes' efforts with Flow Water for his worldwide shows include offsetting carbon emissions, ensuring sustainable catering, donating excess food/toiletries to shelters, and removing the estimated 70 000 plastic bottles found backstage during concerts by replacing them with Flow’s low-impact paper bottles. There will also be programs related to environmental education during his shows.
  4. Pharrell Williams
    • Pharrell Williams who is best known for his number 1 hit, Happy, used his musical talents to convey a message to his audience. He premiered a song called 100 Years: The Song We’ll Only Hear If We Care in efforts to spread the message about their dedications to environmental issues and inspire others to help out as well. That song will officially release in 2117, 100 hears from its cameo. In 2014, he partnered with Climate Reality for a documentary called 24 Hours of Reality and Live Earth: The World is Watching in hopes to spread the message about climate change solutions.
  5. Jaden Smith
    • The 21-year old is not just a talented actor and rapper like his father Will Smith, but is also a young individual who takes out time from his busy life to take action and make awareness about climate change. Jaden Smith is a recipient of an honor from the Environmental Media Association because of his work with Just Water. Jaden Smith is the co-founder of Just Water, which is a company that produces plant based cartons for their sustainability sourced water. In the summer of 2019, he also launched a free, vegan food truck named I Love You to supply healthy and vegan food to homeless people. Jaden Smith also partnered with AI Gore for a global event called 24 Hours of Reality to educate others on climate change and what is being done to prevent it from getting worse.

Now, you don’t have to be famous or rich to help save the planet. To combat climate change, we need as many hands-on-deck as possible. Something as simple as using reusable or recyclable alternatives can help save the planet (and save you some money). Help save the world - read our other blogs to find out how else you can help out.
